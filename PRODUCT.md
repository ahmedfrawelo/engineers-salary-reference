# Product Context

## Product

Engineers Salary Reference is the first module of a broader engineering reference website. It gives engineers a professional place to submit salary data and inspect market ranges by discipline, role, seniority, location, work mode, and company sector.

## Users

- Engineers comparing fair salary ranges before accepting offers.
- Fresh graduates trying to understand market baselines.
- Experienced engineers checking role and sector differences.
- Future admins who may moderate, import, and validate submissions.

## Register

product

## Principles

- The product serves repeated comparison and data entry, not marketing.
- Salary data must be structured, filterable, and easy to scan.
- Anonymous company display should protect contributors when needed.
- The platform should grow beyond salaries without rebuilding architecture.
- UI must feel work-focused, credible, and calm.

## First Module Scope

- Submit a salary report.
- Store submissions in SQL Server.
- Browse submitted salaries.
- Filter by core dimensions.
- Show summary statistics and breakdowns.
